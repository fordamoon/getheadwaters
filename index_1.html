How It Works

One upload. One model. One answer.

Headwaters runs marketing-mix modeling — the method large companies pay analysts six figures to run — tuned for operators with seasonal revenue. It doesn't need tracking pixels or cookies. It needs your history: what you spent, and what you booked.

01

Upload

Export bookings and marketing spend from wherever they live — spreadsheets are fine. Drop the CSV in. No integrations, no IT project.

02

Model

Headwaters maps spend to revenue channel by channel, accounting for seasonality and the multi-touch way customers actually find you.

03

Answer

In about sixty seconds: which channels drove revenue, which didn't, and what we still can't say for sure — in plain language, with error bars.
